Research

Beware: This website is under construction

My postgraduate work has focussed on classifying the 'cosmic web' of large scale structure which we see, and investigating how the properties of structures such as galaxies and halos vary in different regions of the cosmic web.

I am part of the GAMA collaboration. For my thesis I have developed an algorithimn which, starting from the positions of galaxies as measured by the GAMA team, picks out the different parts of the cosmic web. Since we live in 3-dimensions, we can separate structures in to 4 classes, based on the number of collapsed dimensions. I classify structures which have collapsed in 0,1,2 and 3 dimensions as voids, sheets, filaments and knots respectively.
